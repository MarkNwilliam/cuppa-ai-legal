# Privacy Policy for Cuppa AI

**Last Updated: May 8, 2026**

Yee Technologies ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Cuppa AI Google Docs extension (the "Extension"). This Privacy Policy describes how your information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you use our Extension.

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Document Content
To provide the core functionality of turning documents into videos, the Extension accesses the text content of the Google Document you are currently editing. This access is limited to the "current document" only.

### B. User Identity
We collect your Google Email address and profile information to manage your account, track usage limits, and provide customer support.

### C. Technical Data
We may collect anonymized technical data, such as Extension version, browser type, and error logs, to improve the performance and reliability of the service.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- **Service Delivery**: To process your document text and generate video scripts, scenes, and audio.
- **AI Processing**: We transmit document data to secure third-party AI providers (including Google Gemini and Microsoft Azure) to perform high-level content generation.
- **Communication**: To send you important updates regarding the service or respond to support requests.

## 3. Data Protection and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data.
- **Encryption**: All data transmitted between the Extension and our servers is encrypted using TLS/SSL.
- **No Training**: We do not use your document content to train our AI models or third-party models.
- **Access Control**: Only authorized personnel at Yee Technologies have access to support logs.

## 4. Data Retention

- **Ephemeral Processing**: Document text is processed in real-time and is not stored permanently on our servers.
- **Asset Storage**: Generated video files and assets are stored temporarily on secure cloud storage (Azure) and are automatically deleted after 24 hours unless otherwise specified.

## 5. Third-Party Services

Cuppa AI integrates with:
- **Google Workspace APIs**: To interact with your Google Docs.
- **Google Gemini API**: For content analysis and script generation.
- **Microsoft Azure**: For hosting our backend and generating text-to-speech audio.

Each of these providers has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

## 6. Your Rights

Depending on your location (e.g., GDPR in the EU), you may have rights to:
- Access the data we have about you.
- Request deletion of your account and associated data.
- Opt-out of non-essential communications.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
