# Privacy Policy - Cuppa AI

**Last Updated: May 7, 2026**

Yee Technologies ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information through our Google Docs extension, Cuppa AI.

## 1. Information We Collect
To provide the video generation service, Cuppa AI accesses the text content of the Google Document where the extension is active. This content is sent to our backend servers for processing.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
- **Processing**: We use the document text to generate video scripts, visual scenes, and voiceovers.
- **AI Services**: Your content may be processed by third-party AI providers (such as Google Gemini) to generate the video assets. These providers do not use your data to train their models unless explicitly stated otherwise.

## 3. Data Retention
We do not store your document text permanently. It is only held in memory during the generation process. Generated video files are stored temporarily on our secure servers and are deleted after a set period.

## 4. Third-Party Access
We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Data is only shared with essential infrastructure providers (e.g., Azure, Google Cloud) required to run the service.
